FIRE - Fundamental and Interdisciplinary REsearch

FIRE ARAŞTIRMA EĞİTİM LTD. ŞTİ.

FIRE Araştırma Eğitim Ltd. Şti. aspires to advance the borders of humanity's knowledge corpus. It aims to contribute to the global research landscape through rigorous scientific investigation, accessible and free reference materials, and comprehensive training programs. Though FIRE-AE is a start-up limited company, its ambition is to become a beacon of knowledge and discovery in the scientific world.

Provided Solutions

Research


FIRE Araştırma Eğitim Ltd. Şti. conducts independent and interdisciplinary scientific research especially in astrophysics and remote sensing fields. Its projects are driven by curiosity, rigor, and the pursuit of knowledge. Active projects can be seen from Research Projects page.

Training


FIRE Araştırma Eğitim is dedicated to the training of researchers, researcher candidates, and professionals. They will range from undergraduate workshops to graduate-level training programs to key capacity development courses for professionals, all designed to equip the scientists and professionals with the tools they need to succeed, reduce the precarious state of researcher career and empower them, and bring the state-of-the-art to the everyone and teleporting them to the new opportunities. Available training programs can be seen in the Training page.

Reference Materials


FIRE Araştırma Eğitim Ltd. Şti plans to produce high-quality reference materials for both graduate and undergraduate levels, aiming to support and enrich the academic community with available and accessible resources. In addition to the FIRE-AE created resources, compiled high-quality resources can be found Resources page.

Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned researcher, a student beginning the journey in science, or someone demanding a superior level of knowledge/insight in fundamental and applied sciences, there are many ways to get involved with FIRE Araştırma Eğitim Ltd. Şti.:
  • For Researchers: Learn more about FIRE's funding opportunities, training programs, and current research projects.
  • For Students: Explore FIRE's training programs and find out how you can apply.
  • For Service Requesters: You can check out FIRE's services and how it operates, and contact for further details. Not only will you get state-of-the-art insight, you will also contribute to advancing the scientific frontier.
