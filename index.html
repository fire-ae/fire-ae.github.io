FIRE - Fundamental and Interdisciplinary REsearch

FIRE ARAŞTIRMA EĞİTİM LTD. ŞTİ.

FIRE Araştırma Eğitim Ltd. Şti. aspires to advance the borders of humanity's knowledge corpus. It aims to contribute to the global research landscape through rigorous scientific investigation, accessible and free reference materials, and comprehensive training programs. Though FIRE-AE is a start-up limited company, its ambition is to become a beacon of knowledge and discovery in the scientific world.

Research

Training


FIRE Araştırma Eğitim is dedicated to the training of researchers, researcher candidates, and professionals. They will range from undergraduate workshops to graduate-level training programs to key capacity development courses for professionals, all designed to equip the scientists and professionals with the tools they need to succeed, reduce the precarious state of researcher career and empower them, and bring the state-of-the-art to the everyone and teleporting them to the new opportunities.

Available training programs can be seen in the Training page.

An udemy course, Practice and Theory Intro to Satellite Remote Sensing was published by Güray Hatipoğlu, the founder of FIRE-AE.

Many Turkish-spoken videos for satellite remote sensing/Geographic Information System themed concepts by FIRE-AE is available in its YouTube Channel.
