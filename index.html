FIRE - Fundamental and Interdisciplinary REsearch

FIRE Araştırma Eğitim Ltd. Şti. (hereinafter, FIRE-AE) aspires to advance the borders of humanity's knowledge corpus. It aims to contribute to the global research landscape through rigorous scientific investigation, accessible and free reference materials, and comprehensive training programs. Though FIRE-AE is a start-up limited company, its ambition is to become a beacon of knowledge and discovery in the scientific world.
