Outreach – Media & Merch

Selected astrophysical visuals adapted into physical formats for outreach and dissemination. These are small-format previews of printed materials including stickers, cards, and posters. Shipment is available within Türkiye, and it is possible to purchase face-to-face in the following subprovinces of Ankara: Çankaya, Yenimahalle, Etimesgut. Please use the Contact Form at the Home Page for inquiries.

🔭 Stickers

Compact, high-contrast stickers designed for quick recognition and durability, works best with mobile phones and plastic surfaces. Currently, there are two categories of stickers: either taken from public images captured by ground-based or space-based telescopes, or directly captured by Y. Güray Hatipoğlu (sticker will be under one of these two categories and this will be disclosed).

🪐 A6 Cards

Small printed astrophysical plates, suitable for distribution, mailing, and collection. At the request of the purchasing entity, information regarding the plate can be manually written and signed by its creator at the back of the plate. The plates are either directly in A6 size, or a quarter of A4 with/out white edges for easier management.

🌌 Posters (A4–A2)

Larger-format prints of A4, A3, and even A2 for an astonishing addition to your interiors. They will have cool scientific information regarding the astronomical object, the telescope captured it, filters and processing details, as well as the image credits, with FIRE-AE tiny logo at one corner.
