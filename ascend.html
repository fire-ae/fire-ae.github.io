Science Ascend - Biannual Scientific Journal

Biannual journal of FIRE Araştırma Eğitim Ltd. Şti. on recent preprints in astronomy/astrophysics, relevant developments, key upcoming conferences and interesting focus articles, as well as interviews with key people.

This page presents the flagship journal of FIRE-AE, Science Ascend! Science Ascend compiles the preprints from the previous six month in astrophysics from arxiv and discusses them, and that's not all! It will have key information on upcoming confereces/workshops, focus articles on hot topics in space and astrophysics, as well as interviews with key people.
