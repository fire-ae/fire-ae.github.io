Science Ascend

Weekly periodical of FIRE Araştırma Eğitim Ltd. Şti. on recent preprints in astrophysics, chemistry, remote sensing, and data science.

This page presents the flagship journal of FIRE-AE, Science Ascend! Science Ascend compiles the preprints from the previous week in astrophysics, remote sensing, chemistry, and data science/applied statistics domains from arXiv, chemRxiv, and bioRxiv and discusses them. Keep yourself informed of cutting-edge research with this journal!
