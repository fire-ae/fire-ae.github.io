Scientist's Toolbok




This webpage does not provide a "full" list of what is available, but as many as possible. The content here represents freely accessible, no-cost information, medium, data, database, and guidelines to assist in one's scientific research studies. Even though they are fully free and available, they are still of high quality. This is generally done through sponsors and alturistic mechanisms, and occassionally as an advertisement to a much better product. Except the latter case, FIRE will list such resources in this page and this list will be a dynamic, living toolkit for scientists.In case the viewers can have a recommendation where there is a place that can fit in one of the categories below, providing resources freely with no cost, they can inform FIRE-AE about the and FIRE will add those in this page.



Textbooks - Free, Openly Available at no Cost and High-Quality

A major platform where one can reach textbooks prepared by academicians and reach them freely is LibreTexts. As of August 31, 2024 they have 2815 books, mostly in English ranging from agriculture, to differential aquations to workforce fundamentals.

Publicly available, no-cost Data Providers and Data Hubs


Here is a list of data with free access, no-cost, and even permitting commercial use-cases without incurring additional cost. Only sort-of-complicated part is that some hubs require free registration, several token generation and API access via those tokens, but it will take an hour at most to get over this.

Satellite Remote Sensing for Earth Observation

  • OData Service for Sentinel-1, Sentinel-2, Sentinel-3 and Sentinel-5P
  • NASA LP DAAC - Multispectral, Lidar, DEM, Hyperspectral and other RS products
  • EUMETSAT - Meteorology, Climate, SST, Ice and other data products

Environmental Data

  • USGS-NWIS the USA Water Quality Water Flow Data
  • EU Chemical Monitoring Data for Environment, Biota, Food, and Indoor Air
  • EuroStat Waste Stream Data

Manufacture - Production Data

  • EU-wide Manufacture of Goods by NACE code and Product Code - PRODCOM
