2025-01-07 January 07, 2024 - FIRE to Collaborate in Planetary Research Diamond Open Access Journal Project

FIRE Araştırma Eğitim Ltd. Şti. will support Planetary Research, which aims to break the shackles on planetary research community and eliminate the confinement of their research outputs in subscription-based journals or force APC charges. Planetary Research is and will be a diamond open access journal free to both readers and authors.


2024-08-28 August 28, 2024 - FIRE to Support Data Analytics for Impact Professionals by Impact Hub Ankara and Rubiks Analytics

FIRE Araştırma Eğitim Ltd. Şti. will support the training "Data Analytics for Impact Professionals" by Impact Hub and Rubiks Analytics. The training aims to equip impact professionals, even if they have zero experience-info on data analysis, with the techniques and tools used by data scientists to develop their capacity. The webpage for the training is here Güray Hatipoğlu from FIRE can be seen at the bottom of the page among the trainers of this wonderful program. The training program will start no earlier than the end of September, 2024.
